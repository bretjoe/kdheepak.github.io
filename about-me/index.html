About Me

It is surprisingly hard to introduce yourself to people. You (usually) know yourself quite well, and more often that not, you don’t exactly know where to begin. I know I will do no justice to the purpose this page, but nonetheless, an attempt I shall make. I could begin by questioning if a single ‘About Me’ page is enough to provide a legitimate personal history to my sufficient satisfaction, but I’m not entirely comfortable with playing around with the idea of claiming that I’m so interestingly complex that I cannot be summarized on what could be a virtually limitless webpage. I guess I’m just modest like that.

I’m an electrical engineer. I’ve also dabbled with mobile application and web development, home automation and photography. I’m currently working towards a Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering. 

I love visiting and reading up on the history of places. My dream is that one day I’d have travelled to every country in the world. (Four down, 192 to go!) I love watching movies and having discussions with friends about them. I’ve been on the seemingly never ending quest of completing IMDb’s top 250 movies of all time for almost forever. (An embarrassingly small 91 down, 159 to go.) I love reading books and comic books, but I find I’m particularly picky about what I choose to read, especially with the latter. I love technology an extraordinarily unusual amount and even occasionally contribute to a technology news and media network. Check them out here, they are really cool!

On this website, I intend to share interesting projects I’m currently working on or have worked on in the past, also as a way of establishing an archive. I hope to document everything as meticulously as I can. If you find anything interesting, feel absolutely free to email me, or contact me on Facebook, Twitter or Google Plus.
