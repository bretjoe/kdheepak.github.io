AMES Market Test Bed

AMES is a Wholesale Power Market Test Bed. AMES stands for Agent-based Modeling of Electricity Systems. The AMES Market Package is an open source software implementation of the AMES Wholesale Power Market Test Bed in Java. The AMES Market Package version 2.06 was developed at Iowa State University, and is available for download here.
